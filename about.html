About Me Text

Geniusula: The piece of genius who is surrounded by idiots.

After having 3 different university experiences, I came to London. I'm studying Interactive Media Design at Fanshawe College. Since 2013, I've been ignorantly interested in photography from time to time. I've been a writer since I was a kid. I've been writing poetry for 15 years. I have been producing and sharing aphorisms for about 10 years. I decided to combine my interest in photography with my experience of aphorism and set up such a site. Thank you for visiting.

All photos and quotations on the site belong to me.
